Health Behaviours and Perceived Health Across OECD Countries

COS30045 Data Visualisation Assignment - Team 2G | May 14, 2025

This project explores the relationship between health behaviors and perceived health status across OECD countries. From the impact of alcohol consumption to the rise of vaping, regional patterns, and gender differences, we uncover insights through five visualisations that tell a compelling story of public health trends.
